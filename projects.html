Improving Tufts's Student Information System

Students at Tufts University have expressed difficulty when navigating through the existing Student Information System (SIS) on their phone. To pinpoint the pain points of the current SIS, my team and I went out to collect data, conducting user interviews and using surveys to gather results. We consulted with 75 students regarding their issues with the current version and what information or features they wish SIS could provide for their use.

Target User Feedback

From our interviews and surveys, we found that over one-third of the student sample did not even use the mobile SIS website because of their poor first interaction with it. This is due to the incompatible mobile version of the web page as well as the irrelevant information that is presented on the mobile page.
